About Us

We are a registered company in Mexico and the United States with extensive experience specializing in advising people who wish to relocate from other countries and various states of the beautiful Mexican republic. We assist these buyers and investors in locating the ideal properties for their purposes, whether it's a house, condominium, apartment, vacation properties, including auction properties. We also guide them in relation to the location of schools, hospitals, dentists, doctors, banks, public services, and government agencies. We provide assistance on various matters such as obtaining a driver's license, finding a good plumber, and everything necessary to start their new life in the Riviera Maya. All of this is provided to our clients at no cost, guaranteeing accurate work backed by over two decades of experience, based on honesty, transparency, and integrity, during and after the transaction. As a result, they will achieve the financial comfort derived from the secure profits from their monthly rentals and the ever-increasing annual appreciation that is typical of the Riviera Maya. Additionally, due to our many years of work, we have forged working alliances with notaries, lawyers, engineers, architects, construction companies, and banking institutions that help us facilitate the purchase, sale, or construction of properties for our clients in a professional, clear, and accurate manner.
